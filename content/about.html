Helmuth Projects is a website dedicated to the art of needlework. We specialize in crafts such as embroidery, cross stitch, and other needle arts. Our goal is to help people discover the joy and beauty of this timeless art form.

We offer a wide variety of patterns, kits, and supplies for all levels of needleworkers, from beginners to experts. Our collection includes traditional and contemporary designs, and we are constantly updating our inventory with new and exciting projects. We also provide tutorials and tips to help you improve your skills and achieve the best results possible. At Helmuth Projects, we believe that needlework is not just a hobby, but a form of self-expression and creativity. Join us in exploring the world of needle arts and discover the satisfaction of creating something beautiful with your own hands.
