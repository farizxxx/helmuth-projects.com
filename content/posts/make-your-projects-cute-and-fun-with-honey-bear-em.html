What is Applique Stitching?

Applique stitching is a technique where fabric pieces are sewn onto a base fabric to create a design or pattern. This technique can be done by hand or by machine, and is often used in quilting and embroidery projects. Applique stitching is a fun and creative way to add texture, color and interest to any project.

The Beauty of Vintage Stitch Machine

One of the joys of applique stitching is the ability to use vintage stitch machines. These machines not only add a unique touch to your projects, but they also have a rich history and character that cannot be replicated by modern machines. With a vintage stitch machine, you can create timeless designs that are both beautiful and durable.
