When it comes to the state bird of Louisiana, there are actually two different options - the American white pelican and the eastern brown pelican. Both of these beautiful birds are found in Louisiana, and they have become a symbol of the state's unique and diverse natural landscape. The American White Pelican: A Majestic Bird with a Unique Look

The American white pelican is a large bird that is known for its striking appearance. These birds have bright white feathers, a large wingspan, and a long, pointed bill that looks almost like a sword. They are typically found in large flocks, and they can often be seen soaring over lakes and rivers in search of food.
