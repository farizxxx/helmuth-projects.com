It's no surprise that Pikachu, the lovable electric mouse from the Pokemon franchise, made the list. Another beloved character is Naruto Uzumaki, the hero of the Naruto series. Naruto is a powerful ninja who dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. But what makes him special is his never-give-up attitude and his determination to protect his friends and loved ones.

Monkey D. Luffy, the protagonist of One Piece, is another popular anime character. Luffy is a pirate who's on a quest to find the One Piece treasure and become the Pirate King. He also has rubber-like powers that allow him to stretch and contort his body in all sorts of wacky ways.

Two other characters who made the list are Sailor Moon and Goku.
